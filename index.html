From the studio

43 Sunsets is a one-person studio near Chicago. I’ve spent twenty years building sales and operations systems between Japan and the United States — long enough to learn that “it runs” and “it’s ready” are very different things, and that the gap between them is exactly where small businesses get hurt.

So everything on this page ships the unglamorous parts: error handling, idempotency guards, plain-language setup notes, and honest lines about what a tool won’t do.

The name is a small ritual. On the hard days, you watch the sunset — and you count. Careful, deliberate, one at a time. That’s also how these tools are made.
